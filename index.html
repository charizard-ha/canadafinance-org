Why Financial Freedom Matters

Did you know? According to Statistics Canada and Fidelity, the average Canadian household needs approximately $1.7 million to retire comfortably.

Yet most Canadians retire with only a fraction of that saved. Over 40% of Canadians aged 55–64 have less than $250,000 in retirement savings.

Achieving financial independence means freedom from stress, from debt, and from paycheck-to-paycheck living. It's not just about wealth—it's about choices, peace of mind, and a legacy for your family.
